Maps


  • World Map: Depending on selection, the size of each red circle indicates the proportion of COVID tests returning as positive or the number of COVID related deaths per million people.

  • UK Map: The size of each red circle indicates the number of COVID cases as a proportion of the population for each county.
    Scotland, Wales & N. Ireland may have large circles on certain days. This is often due to no reporting on previous days.

  • Sliders: Scroll through historical COVID cases on both maps.


Table


  • Table: Shows the total number of COVID cases and the cases as a Percentage for each UK region.

Charts


  • Total Cases: Total number of UK COVID cases by day.

  • Total Tests: Total number of UK COVID tests by day.

  • % Positive: Proportion of COVID tests with a positive result by day.

  • Hospital Admissions: Total number of UK hospital admissions by day.

  • Hospital Beds: Total number of UK hospital beds occupied by COVID patients.

  • MV Beds: Total number of mechanically ventilated beds occupied by COVID patients in hospital.
